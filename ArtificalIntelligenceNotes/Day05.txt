Adversarial Search
==================

When 2 or more agents are competing or facing each other

Algorithm: minimax(Search)

Player1			Player Score // In the vision of Player1
			------------
Player1 		Player1 is max // Player one trying to be the max score
Player2			Player2 is min // Player two trying to max the player's 1 score the min


Optimization
------------
Alpha-Beta pruning

a >= b		Max // a=alpha b=betta
b <= b		Min
